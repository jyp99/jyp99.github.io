1. Personal Information and Collection Methods

  1. A. The Personal Information We Collect

    The Company collects certain personal information as listed below for PAYCO membership
    registration, effective customer consultations, and in order to provide a variety of services.

    1. - Email addresses, cell phone numbers (PAYCO ID), and passwords

    When a User uses our services, the following types of information can be generated and collected.

    1. - IP Addresses, cookies, dates and times of logins, logs of service usage, logs of usage errors, device information when using mobile services (device model, mobile carrier information, hardware ID, Advertising ID, basic statistics about service usage), information on applications installed in the User's device (including time of installation and run time), payment records, and User’s location information provided that the User has consented to the Terms of the Location Based Service.
    2. - Information collected for identity verification: name, date of birth, gender, encrypted Connection Information (CI), Duplication Information (DI).

      3. When a User makes a payment using PAYCO service, the Company may collect the following information to prevent any unauthorized or illegal use: telephone number, device information (type of device, OS, UUID, IMEI).

  2. B. Personal Information Collection Methods

    The Company collects personal information using the following methods.

    1. - Homepage, mobile devices, paper forms, faxes, phone calls, consultation bulletins, emails,
      entrances for promotions, and requests for shipping
    2. - As provided by partner companies
    3. - Collection using collection tools for generated information

    Also, the Company may collect additional information when a User is using its services, and in such
    cases, the Company will make a separate request to the User to collect and use such personal
    information.

2. Purposes for Collecting and Using Personal Information

The Company utilizes the collected personal information for the following purposes:

  1. - Provide service to Users, verify identification of Users, make purchases, make payments, provide PAYCO transaction record, ship goods and services
  2. - Member management purposes: member registration inquiry, age check and acquisition of consent from legal representative, identity verification of User and legal representative, User identification, member withdrawal inquiry, complaint management, Q&A, etc.
  3. - User protection and operation of service purposes: restrict access of Users who violate the Terms of Use or any relevant statutes, prevent and penalize any unauthorized acts or obstruction of service including use of illegal acts, prevent account theft and illegal transactions, post notification, record maintenance for dispute resolution, etc.
  4. - Provide new service (including individually customized product recommendation service) based on the following: service based on demographics, analysis of access frequency, function improvements, statistics on use of service, service analysis, analysis on User’s product purchases, use of service, interests, and usage records based on statistics, etc.
  5. - Event and promotion purposes: offer information on events, promotions, etc.

3. Duration of Personal Information Storage and Usage

In principle, a User's personal information shall be stored until the User withdraws its registration. However, the Company may store personal information for seven (7) days after a User requests to cancel his or her membership, in order to be prepared for cases in which the cancellation was involuntary due to identity theft. Also, the following items shall be preserved for the specified durations for the following purposes

  1. A. Grounds for Storing Information due to the Company's Internal Policies
  2. B. Grounds for Storing Information Due to Relevant Statutes
    When required to preserve member information in accordance with rules and regulations of relevant statutes, including the Commercial Code and the Act on the Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce, the Company will store member information for a set duration as specified by the related statutes. In such instances, the Company will only use the stored information for the purposes of that storage, and the duration will be as follows.
    1. - Website access logs
      Grounds for preservation: The Protection of Communications Secrets Act
      Duration of preservation: 3 months
    2. - Records related to the handling of customer complaints or disputes
      Grounds for preservation: The Act on the Consumer Protection in the Electronic Commerce
      Duration of preservation: 3 years
    3. - Records of cancelling contracts or subscriptions
      Grounds for preservation: The Act on the Consumer Protection in the Electronic Commerce
      Duration of preservation: 5 years
    4. - Records of payments and the provision of goods and services
      Grounds for preservation: The Act on the Consumer Protection in the Electronic Commerce
      Duration of preservation: 5 years
    5. - Records of e-commerce transaction that exceed KRW 10,000
      Grounds of preservation: Electronic Financial Transactions Act
      Duration of preservation: 5 years
    6. - Records of e-commerce transaction that is equal to or less than KRW 10,000
      Grounds of preservation: Electronic Financial Transactions Act
      Duration of preservation: 1 year
